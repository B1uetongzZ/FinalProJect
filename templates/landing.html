1. The Digital Sleuth: Residual Intelligence

Our forensic brain uses ResNet-18, a deep Convolutional Neural Network (CNN). Unlike traditional models that can "forget" information as they go deeper, ResNet uses Skip Connections (Residual Blocks).

  • Edge Interrogation: Initial layers act like patrol officers, identifying basic tooth boundaries and gum lines.
  • Texture Profiling: Deep layers analyze microscopic pixel patterns, looking for the difference between smooth healthy enamel and the "crusty" topography of calculus.
  • Global Memory: Skip connections allow the AI to compare tiny details against the "big picture" of the tooth simultaneously, preventing evidence corruption.
